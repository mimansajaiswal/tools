    How to read this breakdown

    • All values are cumulative through each column date.
    • Salary Cash: after-tax base pay after 401k, Backdoor IRA, ESPP, and Mega Backdoor Roth payroll contributions.
    • Investment Delta: after-tax salary premium invested in Company B stock.
    • RSU: value of vested shares kept after sell-to-cover taxes.
    • ESPP: market value of purchased shares at exit; purchase cost is already deducted from Salary Cash.
    • Backdoor IRA: post-tax contribution converted to Roth IRA and compounded at 5% until exit.
    • 401k Match: employer match future value at 5% growth.
    • Mega Backdoor Roth: after-tax 401k contribution value under the 415(c) limit; no extra growth assumed.
    • Cash (Sign-on + Bonus): after-tax sign-on and bonus paid as cash. Invested Sign-on + Bonus: that same after-tax cash invested in Company B stock.

    Assumptions: partner pre-tax 401k input reduces household federal taxable income; 401k match uses the IRS compensation limit ($360k in 2026, +$5k/year).
